About QOVA:

QOVA is a online tool designed to streamline the process of creating, sharing & evaluating surveys. Topic of these surveys are the different courses of the TU Dresden.

Access to creating courses & surveys is limited to employes of the TU Dresden. New surveys can be shared by downloading the QR-Codes on the course page. Once scanned by the students they will get redirected to the online survey. Afterwards the results can be checked on the 'Survey Results' page.
