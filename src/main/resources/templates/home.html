About QOVA

QOVA is a project initiated at the faculty of medicine. QOVA aims to make the evaluation process more transparent for students as well as mentors and lecturers. By publishing results publicly, a more detailed picture can be painted of how relevant, engaging and informative subjects at the TU Dresden actually are, as evaluated by students.

Beta Version

The current beta 0.1 version was launched at the beginning of the winter semester 2020/2021. We are currently working on improvements for both students and teachers, with a few features in the works for a release during the summer semester 2021. We appreciate any and all feedback!
